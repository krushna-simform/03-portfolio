# 03-portfolio

## Based on the concepts I have learned in this section (components, props, states, etc.), I will create a portfolio website that includes the following details in individual sections:

- A unique and brief introduction about myself

- A showcase of the projects I have created so far

- My skills and areas of expertise

- My educational background

- My hobbies and interests

- A contact form where users can reach out to me. The form will include input fields for:

  - Name

  - Email

  - Contact number (validated to ensure it's 10 digits)

  - A text area labeled "How can I help?"

Upon submission, I will receive an email notification. The form will be properly validated, ensuring that the email format is correct and the contact number is exactly 10 digits.

Additionally, I will include a section for my social media handles, such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and others.
